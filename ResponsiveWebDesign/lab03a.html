Summary of Portugal

Portugal is a country located in Southern Europe. It was founded in 1143. It's capital is Lisbon and the language spoken there is Portuguese. The fact that is it on the Atlantic Ocean influenced any aspects including its culture, food etc... For example, a main food source is fish. Economically at its peak it was the richest country but in the 19th/20th century it had a downfall. Portugal is now one of the poorest. Although, it is still a known place for tourists to come see, and is also known for its football/soccer team.
