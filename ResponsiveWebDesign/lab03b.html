Summary of Portugal

Portugal is a country located in Southern Europe. It was founded in 1143. Its capital is Lisbon and the language spoken there is Portuguese. The fact that it is on the Atlantic Ocean influenced many aspects including its culture, food, etc. For example, a main food source is fish. Economically, at its peak, it was the richest country but in the 19th/20th century, it had a downfall. Portugal is now one of the poorest. Although, it is still a known place for tourists to visit, and is also famous for its football/soccer team.

Portugal is the most western point in Europe!
More than 8 different countries speak Portugal's language!
Portugal is known for its fine wine!
Its population is more than 10 million!
